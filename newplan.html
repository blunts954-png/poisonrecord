This playbook provides a complete sales strategy for acquiring small businesses in Bakersfield's downtown (93301) and White Lane & Union (93309) corridors. The approach combines systematic cold outreach with industry-specific messaging, targeting businesses with under 50 employees that either lack websites or operate with fragmented digital systems.

The strategy emphasizes zero-cost digital tactics, sub-$50 targeted advertising, and a 21-day follow-up sequence designed to identify pain points before recommending appropriate integrated website + POS solutions. The methodology prioritizes relationship-building over transactional selling, with conversion rates anticipated to exceed industry averages due to hyper-local focus and solution specificity.

Objection Handling Framework

"I already have a website"

Probe functionality: "When you pull it up on your phone, how does it look? Does it connect to your operations?"

"Too expensive"

Reframe as consolidation: "What are you spending now on website, POS, and processing all together?"

"No time to switch"

Emphasize done-for-you: "Your involvement is maybe two hours total. We handle migration and setup."

"Need to think about it"

Create urgency: "I can only onboard two more Bakersfield businesses this month..."

Technical Requirements Gathering

Transaction Volume

Daily/weekly sales, average ticket, peak periods

"Walk me through a typical week—what's your busiest day?"

Inventory Complexity

SKU count, variants, supplier relationships

"How do you track what you have in stock? What happens when you run low?"
