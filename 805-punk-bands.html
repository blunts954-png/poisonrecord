What Makes 805 Punk Different

805 punk carries a raw mix of coastal SoCal speed and working-class grit, shaped by small venues, DIY bills, and community-first support. The scene overlaps with Nardcore roots while keeping a local identity built on live intensity and direct storytelling. Poison Well Records treats this as a living archive, not just a reissue lane.
