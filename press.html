The Journey of The Pitts

Ten Years of Silence. One Decade of Sound.

The Pitts are not a "new" band; they are a decade-long evolution finally reaching its apex.

Hailing from Ventura, California, the quartet has spent the last ten years refining a sound they describe as "High-Intensity Dad Rock"—a defiant reclamation of the lyric-driven, melody-heavy rock that once dominated the airwaves.

Fronted by Matt Hoover, The Pitts deliver a visceral blend of 90s alternative grit and anthemic blue-collar punk. Their music occupies the space between the storytelling of The Replacements and the modern urgency of The Gaslight Anthem.

Their debut full-length album, Jamestown (2026), is a record forged in the fires of personal loss and creative perseverance. From the soaring, grief-stricken heights of the title track to the raw, driving energy of "Break Me Down," The Pitts prove that while trends fade, authentic rock and roll is immortal.

Poison Well Mission

Preservation. Documentation. Distribution.

Poison Well Records is a working archive dedicated to the 805 punk and hardcore story.

Founded in Ventura, California, Poison Well exists to bridge the gap between regional memory and physical media. We specialize in limited vinyl pressings, archival reissues, and the digital preservation of the sounds that defined the Southern California underground.

Every release in our catalog is curated with an eye toward historical accuracy and production quality. We don’t just sell records; we document the scene history for the people who lived it and the ones who are just arriving.
