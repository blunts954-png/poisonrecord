Poison Well Records FAQ

Answers about Ventura punk vinyl, 805 bands, shipping, submissions, and the label's archive mission.

What is Poison Well Records?

Poison Well Records is a punk record label and online store focused on 805 and SoCal punk rock, based around the Ventura scene.

Where is Poison Well Records located?

Poison Well Records operates out of Ventura, California and works with bands across the 805 and SoCal punk scenes.

Does Poison Well Records only release 805 bands?

The label focuses on 805 and SoCal punk, but also supports related punk and hardcore bands that share the same spirit.

Does Poison Well Records sell vinyl online?

Yes, current vinyl releases are available to order directly from the online shop, with shipping options for the U.S. and international customers.

How do I get my band on Poison Well Records?

Bands can contact the label via the contact form with links to music, live footage, and background info.

What kind of music does Poison Well Records release?

The label focuses on punk, hardcore, and 805/SoCal-adjacent sounds from classic 1980s material to new underground releases.

Does Poison Well Records ship internationally?

Yes. Poison Well ships worldwide from Ventura, California.

Can record shops stock Poison Well releases wholesale?

Yes. Reach out for wholesale pricing and distro info through the contact and wholesale page.

Is Poison Well tied to Ventura punk history?

Yes. Ventura is the label's anchor for archive work, releases, and community context.

Do you release digital formats too?

Yes. Selected releases are distributed digitally alongside vinyl when applicable.

How can I hear about limited pressings first?

Join the mailing list for first access to new drops, test pressings, and event updates.

Do you archive old demos and live recordings?

Yes, when source quality and rights are available, Poison Well works to preserve and reintroduce important scene recordings.

What is Nardcore and how is it related to Ventura punk?

Nardcore is a Ventura County hardcore punk movement tied to fast, aggressive DIY sounds. Poison Well's catalog is Nardcore adjacent through Ventura and 805 roots.

How much does Poison Well Records charge for shipping?

Shipping cost depends on destination, package weight, and order size. Final rates are shown during checkout for U.S. and international orders.

What vinyl formats does Poison Well Records offer?

Poison Well offers LPs, EPs, 7-inch releases, and occasional color variants when available.

Can I buy Poison Well Records vinyl at local record stores?

Some titles are available through partner shops and wholesale accounts. Contact Poison Well for current store availability.

Does Poison Well Records repress sold-out vinyl?

Some sold-out titles may be repressed depending on demand, rights, and source material. Join the email list for repress announcements.

What is the difference between 805 punk and SoCal punk?

805 punk is a regional lane within broader SoCal punk, often with tighter Ventura/Oxnard scene ties and strong DIY community overlap.

How do I track my Poison Well Records order?

After purchase, tracking information is sent by email when your order ships. Contact support if you need tracking updates.
