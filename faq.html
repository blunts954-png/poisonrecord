Poison Well Records FAQ

Answers about Ventura punk vinyl, 805 bands, shipping, submissions, and the label's archive mission.

THE MANIFESTO

Poison Well Records is an archival-grade punk label. Our mission: the preservation and distribution of 805 and SoCal underground history.

GROUND ZERO

Operations are localized in Ventura, CA. We serve as the primary link for the 805 and SoCal punk trajectory.

SHIPPING PROTOCOLS

Domestic (US): Standard shipping via USPS Media Mail. Expected delivery 5-9 business days.

International: Worldwide shipping active via USPS First Class International. Rates calculated at secure checkout.

GRADING & QUALITY

All archive vinyl is graded using the Goldmine Standard. New pressings are strictly Mint (M) / Near Mint (NM). Archive drops include detailed condition reports in the rarity vault.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

All sales from the Rarity Vault are final due to the limited nature of archival units. For standard distribution, defects must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.

PRESERVATION PROTOCOL

Affirmative. We actively recover and restore high-priority scene recordings where rights and source quality meet archival standards.
