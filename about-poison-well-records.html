About Poison Well Records

So Cal and 805 punk rock, archived for the forgotten youth.

Origin Story

Poison Well Records started to preserve Ventura and 805 punk history before tapes, flyers, and recordings disappeared. The mission is scene memory: give bands and fans a reliable home for physical releases and documented context.

The label is built from direct connection to both fans and bands, with a focus on keeping regional stories accurate and accessible.

What Poison Well Does

  • Releases limited-run vinyl and digital for 805 / SoCal punk bands.
  • Archives live sets, demos, and long-lost recordings when possible.
  • Ships worldwide from Ventura and works directly with bands or estates when applicable.
