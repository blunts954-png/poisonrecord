POISON WELLRECORDS

PRIVACY POLICY

Effective: April 2024

TRANSPARENCY & DATA

Poison Well Records values the privacy of the underground community. We collect the minimal amount of information required to process and ship your orders, typically limited to your name, shipping address, and contact information.

PAYMENT PROCESSING

We do not store your financial information. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. When you checkout, your data is transmitted over an encrypted SSL connection directly to their servers. We never see your credit card details.

COOKIES & TRACKING

Our site uses essential cookies to manage your shopping cart and maintain session state. We do not use intrusive cross-site tracking or sell your behavioral data to advertisers. We're here for the music, not your metadata.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We work with trusted partners like Netlify and Stripe to host and operate this platform. These services have their own privacy policies which govern how they handle your data within their infrastructure.

COMMUNITY EMAILS

If you choose to contact us or sign up for updates, we will only use your email for relevant communication regarding your orders or Poison Well archive updates. You can opt-out at any time.

CONTACT

For any questions regarding your information or to request data removal, please reach out to poisonwellrecords@gmail.com.
