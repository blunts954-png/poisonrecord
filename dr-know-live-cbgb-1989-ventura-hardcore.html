About Dr. Know and Ventura Hardcore

Dr. Know helped define Ventura hardcore through speed, precision, and a relentless live presence that connected the local scene to a broader SoCal punk network. The 1989 CBGB recording captures that crossover moment when 805 intensity hit a legendary East Coast stage. This pressing preserves a factual snapshot of Ventura hardcore history for current listeners and longtime scene veterans.
